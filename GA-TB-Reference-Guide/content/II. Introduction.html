There is no "gold standard" test for latent TB infection (LTBI). Two types of diagnostic tests to detect LTBI are now available. These include the tuberculin skin test (TST) which has been avail- able for > 100 years and a newer generation of diagnostic tests, the interferon-Î³ release assays (IGRAs), which are T-cell-based in - vitro blood tests. Two IGRAs are approved for use by the U.S. FDA. Neither the TST nor IGRA tests can distinguish LTBI from active TB disease.
