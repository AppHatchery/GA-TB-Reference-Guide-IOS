750 mg
Frequency Daily
Adverse Reactions GI upset, dizziness, hypersensitivity, Headaches, QT prolongation, tendon rupture (rare), arthralgia, increased risk for aortic dissection/rupture, hypo/hyperglycemia

Additional Notes:

Dosing above for persons with CrCL > 50 mL/min. For persons with renal impairment, see Table 13. Expert consultation is advised before use; agent is not approved for children less than 18 years of age.
